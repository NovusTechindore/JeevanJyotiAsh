Home School

Along with Spiritual activities we the monks promote quality Education for people around. The apostolate of education in St Benedict’s School started in 1998 with just four classrooms and now we have about 2500 students in St. Benedict’s managed by Jeevan Jyoti Ashram. For further developments and spread of Benedictine presence we look forward to start a house at Palwal in Haryana State in North India near New Delhi.
